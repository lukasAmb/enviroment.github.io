Why Littering is Bad

Littering has numerous negative impacts on the environment and society. It pollutes our land, water, and air, harming wildlife and ecosystems. Additionally, litter can be hazardous to human health and safety, leading to injuries and spreading diseases. It also reduces the aesthetic appeal of our communities and can have economic consequences, costing taxpayers millions of dollars each year for cleanup efforts.

By properly disposing of waste and recycling whenever possible, we can help mitigate these problems and create a cleaner, healthier environment for current and future generations.
