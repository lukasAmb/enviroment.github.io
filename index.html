Why Littering is Bad

Littering is a significant environmental issue that negatively impacts our planet. When waste is not disposed of properly, it can harm wildlife, pollute waterways, and degrade natural landscapes.

Wildlife often mistakes litter for food, which can lead to ingestion of harmful materials and even death. Additionally, litter can block drainage systems, causing flooding and other problems in urban areas.

By reducing litter, we can protect our ecosystems, maintain the beauty of our surroundings, and promote a healthier environment for future generations. Everyone has a role to play in keeping our planet clean.
